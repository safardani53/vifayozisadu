    So far, the 2022 Grammy Awards have included a number of A-list singers, including Billie Eilish, who sang Happier Than Ever. This is the 20-year-third old’s time attending the music awards presentation. Fans praised Eilish not just for her performance but also for paying respect to the late Taylor Hawkins. The drummer for the Foo Fighters died on March 25th, at the age of 50, when the band was on tour in Bogota, Colombia.

    Billie Eilish’s delicate tribute to the late Taylor Hawkins is praised by fans.

    The first thing the crowd noticed when Billie Eilish took the stage at the 2022 Grammy Awards was her T-shirt. Her touching homage to the late Hawkins touched many fans’ hearts. Netizens commended the artist for honoring the drummer’s memory throughout the musical evening. Here are a few of their responses:

    Taylor Hawkins died in his hotel room last month after complaining of chest trouble. The death was revealed on social media by the Foo Fighters. According to reports, the band was scheduled to play at the Estereo Picnic Festival in Bogota, Colombia. Hawkins is survived by his wife Alison and their three children.

    The Foo Fighters have received numerous nominations for the 2022 Grammy Awards.

    The 64th Grammy Awards aired on CBS on Sunday, April 3 at 8 p.m. Taylor Hawkins’ Foo Fighters are nominated for many awards. The band has received nominations for Best Pop Vocal Album, Best Rock Album, and Best Rock Song. Billie Eilish, on the other hand, has been nominated for seven awards. Record of the Year, Album of the Year, Song of the Year, Best Pop Solo Performance, Best Music Video, Best Pop Vocal Album, and Best Music Film are among the categories. The award event is attended by a slew of celebrities. BTS, John Legend and his wife Chrissy Teigen, Bruno Mars, Justin Bieber, Nas, Silk Sonic, Leslie Odom Jr., Brothers Osborne, Dua Lipa, and Lady Gaga are among them.

    Some celebrities attend the event to perform, while others go to give an award. The musical event, hosted by Trevor Noah, is presently streaming live on CBS and Paramount Plus. Viewers may also get the program via a variety of streaming services, including Fubo TV, Hulu + Live TV, YouTube TV, Sling, Philo, and DirecTV Stream.
