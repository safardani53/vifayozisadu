    IDRIS Elba and Gemma Arterton star in some racy naked sex scenes for their new movie 100 streets.

    The film follows on an ex-England rugby captain, played by Idris, who is fond of drugs and women and Gemma plays his estranged ex-wife, who he briefly reconciles with.

    In the flick, Idris's character Max Moore is struggling to cope as his life spirals out of control after he was caught having an affair with the nanny.

    He is also finding it hard to deal with being out of spotlight following his sporting career.

    Meanwhile, former Bond girl Gemma plays his scorned wife Emily who is trying to come to terms with her husband's cheating ways and find new direction in her life after the split.

    Idris, 44, spoke candidly about filming the intimate bedroom scenes, which happen after the pair have a brief moment of weakness and appear to reconcile.

    He said: "Love scenes though are definitely challenging, Gemma is a pro and [director] Jim O'Hanlon gave us a skeleton set, so it just made it a lot easier to do. But it's always difficult to do."

    Meanwhile, Idris wasn't only getting up close and personal with Gemma as The Sun exclusively revealed last week that he was flirting with supermodel Jourdan Dunn at the EMAs.

    The pair flirted up a storm at the after-party before leaving hand-in-hand.

    They had earlier both presented awards during the ceremony at the Ahoy Rotterdam arena in Holland, before heading off to the backstage bash where they stayed well into the early hours of Monday morning.

    An eyewitness at the party said: “The VIP area was swarming with celebrities keen to let down their hair after the ceremony.

    “Idris took a table tucked away in the corner with comfy sofas and was talking to a number of girls earlier in the night.

    “But Jourdan joined his table later on in the evening and he was clearly interested in her.

    “He was very flirty and was acting very much like a single man. At about 2.30am they left holding hands.”

    100 Streets is out now.
