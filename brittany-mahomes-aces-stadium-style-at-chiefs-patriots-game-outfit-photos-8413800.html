    See Brittany Mahomes Outfit at Chiefs vs. Patriots Game

    For Brittany Mahomes, F stands for football and fashion! Mahomes, 28, always pulls out all the style stops to cheer on her husband, Kansas Chief quarterback Patrick Mahomes, at stadiums across the country and her latest look might be her chicest yet.

    27 Aug 2024, 00:00

    For Brittany Mahomes, “F” stands for football — and fashion! 

    Mahomes, 28, always pulls out all the style stops to cheer on her husband, Kansas Chief quarterback Patrick Mahomes, at stadiums across the country — and her latest look might be her chicest yet.

    At Sunday's matchup between the Chiefs and the New England Patriots, the mom of two — and seasoned style pro — teamed a black mini skirt with matching turtleneck, thigh-high boots and an ivory coat customized with "Mahomes" in black block letters across the back. She topped off the look with a red top handle Dior box bag to nod to the team's colors.

    She shared her full look with a field-side Instagram snap.

    Brittany joined Taylor Swift, who’s dating Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce, as the duo cheered in a box at Sunday's game at the Patriots’ home turf in Foxborough, Massachusetts. Swift teamed her gray Chiefs sweatshirt with a red-and-white knit beanie with Kelce's number on it.

    Last week Brittany supported her man from the sidelines at Arrowhead Stadium and shared a sweet moment with her and the NFL player's 12-month-old son, Patrick "Bronze" Lavon, on the field.

    In a photo shared to Mahomes' Instagram Story, the newborn was all decked out in Chiefs merchandise. Like his mom — who wore red leather pants and a black puffer vest — Bronze sported red with a striped track jacket and a beanie.

    "Mood," Mahomes wrote over the image.

    By now, Brittany has got her sideline style down pat and every one of her Chiefs uniforms have earned a 10/10 score. 

    At the Chiefs game in Green Bay, Brittany dressed casually in a sporty varsity jacket while the Swift, 34, wore a red Stella McCartney teddy bear coat, which eagle-eyed fans noticed was nearly identical to the fuzzy trench Mahomes wore not too long ago. (Mahomes did, after all, channel the super star’s “Fearless” curls back in October.) 

    Prior to that, at the Chiefs game against the Philadelphia Eagles, the Whitehouse, TX native decided to rep the white portion of Patrick’s team’s colors. 

    Her outfit included some major on-trend pieces, including a cropped puffer coat and cargo pants with red patches and emblems of Patrick’s jersey number.
