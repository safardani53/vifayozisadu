    Who is Ishowspeed's girlfriend Ermony Renee?

    IShowSpeed introduced his followers to his girlfriend, Ermony Renee, in 2021 during a live stream.

    It was revealed the couple had met at her local United Dairy Farmers shop and quickly became an item.

    Ermony hails from Cincinnati, Ohio, and studied at Walnut Hills High School before going on to attend the University of Cincinnati.

    She has over 10k followers on her Instagram account, which has the username @ermonyy.

    IShowSpeed and Ermony's romance has been described as on-again-off-again by some reports.

    How did IShowSpeed become successful?

    IShowSpeed, whose real name is Darren Watkins Jr, worked in a nursing home delivering food to the elderly before embarking on his YouTube career.

    In 2019 he began uploading his gaming content but he really rose to fame during the Covid-19 lockdown.

    When the pandemic began he began uploading more content because he was bored and gained a lot of recognition in a small amount of time.
