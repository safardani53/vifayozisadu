    RHONY veterans are reuniting on The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip and their season 4 filming location may be the best hotspot yet.

    Sandy beaches, here we come! The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip is jetting off to another tropical location for season 4 and this time, they’ve recruited the most outspoken characters of The Real Housewives Of New York. Glitz, glam, and luxury are guaranteed for the ladies of the Big Apple in their 2023 holiday hotspot.

    The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip season 4 filming location

    The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip cast – Kelly Killoren Bensimon, Luann de Lesseps, Dorinda Medley,
    Sonja Morgan, Ramona Singer, and Kristen Taekman – are heading to St Barts for the first time on Peacock.

    The Caribbean island is a celebrity hotspot thanks to its white-sand beaches and designer shops, so it’s heaven on earth for the Bravo stars. Famous faces who have flocked to the island paradise include Leonardo DiCaprio and The Kardashians.

    Even RHONY alumna Bethenny Frankel was spotted splashing in St Barts waters in February 2023, four months before season 4 filming was underway. Unfortunately, she won’t be appearing in RHUGT, despite fan rumors speculating they heard her in cast TikToks. The good news is Luann teased the new season will be “Scary Island Part II” – the iconic RHONY episode where Kelly had a “breakdown” and was convinced Bethenny was out to get her.

    St Barts served as the backdrop of the RHONY season 5 cast trip. And yes, they’ll also be lodging in the infamous house on Saline Beach, aka the Pirate House where Luann flirted with the Johnny-Depp lookalike “pirate”. Unfortunately, nothing more happened between De Lesseps and her summer fling.

    RHUGT vacation destinations over the years

    Season 1

    View Instagram Post

    The premiere season of RHUGT was based in Turks and Caicos, starring Cynthia Bailey, Luann de Lesseps, Teresa Giudice, Melissa Gorga, Kenya Moore, Kyle Richards, and Ramona Singer. The ladies relaxed in the Triton Luxury Villa, where prices start at $8000 for seven bedrooms for a minimum three-day stay.

    Season 2

    View Instagram Post

    Season 2 stayed close to home – specifically Dorinda Medley’s Blue Stone Manor in Great Barrington, Massachusetts. Titled Ex-Wives Club, it starred Housewives who were no longer cast members on their original shows.

    Medley’s home is built on 18 acres and boasts seven bathrooms and seven bedrooms. One year before filming, Blue Stone Manor suffered a severe flood after a “tremendous cold spell” broke the pipes above her heating system. Damages reportedly totaled to $1 million.

    Season 3

    View Instagram Post

    RHUGT’s biggest cast yet came in season 3 as they flew to the other side of the world to Phuket, Thailand. Featuring the likes of Candiace Dillard Baasset, Porsha Williams Guobadia, and Heather Gay, there were a total of eight Bravo stars living in Villa Aye, which sits on “Phuket’s ‘Millionaire’s Mile'”.

    Prices for the eight-bedroom, three-level property start at 9894 for a three-night stay.
