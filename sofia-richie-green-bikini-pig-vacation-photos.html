    Sofia Richie, 22, escaped to a beach paradise! Lionel Richie‘s youngest daughter looked sensational as she posed in the green “Praia” bikini Tropics Of C ($150) from the unknown destination on Saturday, Nov. 22. Looking out into the turquoise blue water, she was joined by two pigs hanging out on both sides of her — suggesting she’s likely in the Bahamas. “Pigs in Paradise,” she captioned her multi-photo post including a sea shell emoji.

    In the next shot, the Rolla’s Jeans model walked along the sandy beach with her wet hair slicked back rocking a vintage pair of Celine’s orange wireless sunglasses to protect her eyes. She showed off her toned abs and toned body in the sexy two-pice swimsuit as the pigs led the way! Her longtime friend Abby Smidt also joined in on the vacation, twinning in a green swimsuit for the next shot, followed by a video of Sofia swimming with the friendly pigs. Hilariously, Abby appeared to be trying to get a trepidatious Sofia to pet one of the brownish-pink animals!

    The getaway comes just a week after Sofia’s ex-boyfriend Scott Disick, 37, appears to have moved on romantically with model Amelia Hamlin, 19. Rumors began swirling that the Talentless streetwear designer and Lisa Rinna‘s daughter were dating after they were seen heading to a Halloween party together on Oct. 31. The pair seemingly confirmed the news with a PDA-filled beach stroll in Malibu, California on Nov. 16. Scott draped his arm around a bikini-clad Amelia as they walked along the surf, later dining out the same evening at Tony Taverna’s in the Malibu Country Mart.

    Since splitting with Scott for good over the summer, Sofia has also moved on. The 22-year-old has been spotted on several dates with Matthew Morton, 27, who is the younger brother of the late Harry Morton. Sofia and the Cha Cha Matcha founder shared a romantic kiss on Nov. 2 in Beverly Hills while dining out with friends. Sofia’s family, including her brother Miles Richie, 26, is also a fan of her new relationship! “Sofia’s brother approves [of Morton] which is a huge bonus, because his opinion means everything to her,” a source spilled to HollywoodLife EXCLUSIVELY, adding that the fling is “nothing serious right now.”

    As for Scott and Sofia, the pair have seen each other since their final split — including a run-in at Kendall Jenner‘s Halloween-themed birthday bash. “Sofia is still friends with Kylie, Kendall, and countless other people that are also friends with Scott. They’ve hung in the same social circles forever and so that doesn’t just go away because they broke up,” another source told HL after the potentially awkward interaction. “Sofia is very mature and she knows how to handle herself, and isn’t worried about running into Scott. She knows it’s bound to happen one way or another. She’s not the type of person to avoid social situations because an ex may or may not be there.”
