    Kyle Richards Hits Gym on Mexico Trip with Faye Resnick

    10 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Even in the midst of a lavish spa getaway, Kyle Richards made time to hit the gym.

    “Working out while on vacation 😱,” the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star shared on her Instagram stories, highlighting how the lush, tropical resort was visible through the windows of the gym at Auberge Resort Susurros del Corazón in Punta Mita, Mexico.

    Richards shared that she ran for nearly 5 miles, burning more than 660 calories, on the treadmill’s “leg sculpt plus” setting — a post she embellished with a gif of herself in a hot pink strappy top, rolling her eyes and checking her nails.

    But when not at the gym, Richards, 54, and close friend (and sometimes RHOBH guest star) Faye Resnick, 66, are taking advantage of the resort’s amenities.

    “So needed this unexpected spa trip to Paradise before Christmas with my girl 🌴☀️🌴,” Resnick wrote on a poolside Instagram post, where she has what appears to be a jalepeño margarita in her lap, along with Lisa See’s 2023 historical novel, Lady Tan’s Circle of Women. 

    Richards, who split from husband Mauricio Umansky in July, shared video of a tranquil pool that she captioned “the spa and relaxation room,” and on Tuesday, shared a selfie of herself on the beach, captioned, “Sometimes we just need to escape reality for a bit 🌞 🌊 📚.” 

    Meanwhile, Umansky, 53, is experiencing a very different climate. He shared on his Instagram story his time in the snow — possibly in Aspen, Colorado, where the couple own a home together. In one image, he was enjoying the outdoors with one of the family’s dogs.

    “Ski in the morning hike in the afternoon, reflect, perfect day,” the real estate mogul wrote over the image.

    He also shared footage of himself skiing with singer Anitta, and YouTuber LeLe Pons — while they were clad in towels and drinking champagne from the bottle — for an Instagram video. 

    These trips come after the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star opened up about the future of her marriage. In the franchise’s mid-season trailer, Richards is shown contemplating if her 27-year marriage is over.

    "I want to feel happy and fulfilled. I don't know if Moe and I are going to make it,” she confessed.

    Richards has previously said she was the driving force behind their split.

    During an October appearance on Watch What Happens Live, Richards told host Andy Cohen, "It originated from me, I’ll say."
