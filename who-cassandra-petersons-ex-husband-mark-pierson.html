    20 May 2024, 00:00

    CASSANDRA Peterson has come out in her new memoir, Yours Cruelly, Elvira: Memoirs Of The Mistress Of The Dark.

    The actress revealed that she was never interested in women before her marriage ended, so let’s take a look at who Mark Pierson is and why their relationship ended. 

    Who is Cassandra Peterson's ex-husband Mark Pierson? 

    Mark was born on September 6, 1955 in Los Angeles, California.

    He is a singer, producer and manager with an estimated net worth of $1million. 

    He has a number of producer credits including Elvira’s Haunted Hills (2001), The Elvira Show (1993), Midnight Madness (1990) and Elvira: Mistress of the Dark (1988).

    He also has one acting credit in an Elvira show, playing the butler in Elvira's Haunted Hills.

    Mark wrote Elvira's Theme which features on the 1988 Elvira: Mistress of the Dark Soundtrack.

    How long was Cassandra Peterson married to Mark Pierson for?

    Mark and Cassandra tied the knot in 1981 and he became her personal manager.

    The couple worked together on numerous Elvira shows.

    They had one daughter together, Sadie, who was born on October 12, 1994.

    Sadie has had a keen interest in music since her childhood and sings as well as plays the guitar. 

    However, Mark and Cassandra's relatioship didn't last and they finally divorced on February 13, 2003.

    More on Celebrity news

    Why did Cassandra Pierson split with Mark Pierson?

    Fans were convinced this couple were going to be together forever as they came across to the public "so in love" with each other. 

    After 22 years of marriage and a daughter the pair made the hard decision to part ways.

    Their divorce was finalized in 2003 and the reason behind it has still not been revealed.

    However, Cassandra has revealed in her new memoir, that she has been together with her now partner Teresa Wierson for 19 years.
