    TEEN Mom Briana DeJesus has shocked fans by announcing: "We are having a baby," after fighting with her two baby daddies, Luis Hernandez and Devoin Austin.

    Briana, 28, shared the baby news as she posed with her gal pals in an Instagram photo. 

    The group appeared to be standing outside in a parking lot.

    The Teen Mom 2 star wore a denim jacket over her stunning white floral dress.

    The MTV star smiled at the camera as she stood next to her pregnant "bestie" Shirlz.

    Briana captioned the post: "We are having a baby!"

    More Briana DeJesus

    In the first Instagram Story, the reality star revealed she had attended a gender reveal party in Orlando, Florida. 

    Briana wrote: "My bestie is having a baby.

    "And it's a girl," with a teary-eyed emoji. 

    Briana shares daughter Nova, 10, with Devoin, 29, and Stella, four, with her other ex Luis Hernandez.

    DEVOIN'S RANT

    Recently, Devoin's deleted rant against the MTV network resurfaced on a Teen Mom fan page.

    In the Instagram Story, he ranted: "Count on MTV to do some unprofessional s**t.

    "Play with yo b**ch. Play with yo mama, but don't play with me."

    In a previous episode of the reality show, Briana brought up how Luis has been absent from his daughter Stella’s life.

    She said: "He said he's sorry. He's going to do better, like every other conversation."

    Roxanne, Briana's mother, mentioned that Stella has a "great" relationship with Devoin but noted: "That's not her father."
