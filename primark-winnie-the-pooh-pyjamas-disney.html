    IF we had to name our two ultimate guilty pleasures, it would have to be classic Disney movies swiftly followed by brunch.

    Well for the first time ever, Primark's latest pyjama offering has combined the two... and we're going to be snapping up this bargain buy ASAP.

    Complete with a snuggly £6 red t-shirt and a cute £8 pair of bow-tie bottoms, these Winnie the Pooh pjs are the stuff of our Disney dreams.

    After all, the lazy and lovable teddy bear is effectively our spirit animal when it comes to getting out of bed in the mornings.

    Plus we couldn't agree with Pooh more when he says "no body gets in the way of me and my brunch..." before dunking his head into a pot of honey.

    Anyone else struggling to recall that passage in the A. A. Milne books..?

    What's more, there's also an adorable button-down bed shirt included in the range which costs a mere £6.

    As well a super sweet tank top and shorts set featuring the likes of Eeeyore and Tigger.

    Honestly, if these pjs don't get your day off on the right foot, we don't know what will... maybe a spot of honey?
