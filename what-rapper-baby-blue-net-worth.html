    What is rapper Baby Blue's net worth?

    Diamond “Baby Blue” Smith, 36, has a reported net worth of $10million.

    The rapper made a fortune as a founding member of Miami group Pretty Ricky.

    Pretty Ricky’s biggest single, Grind With Me became a platinum certified single and peaked at the number 7 spot on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

    Blue is also the son of Bluestar Entertainment CEO Joseph "Blue" Smith.

    What does Baby Blue do now?

    Blue has been part of Love and Hip Hop: Miami since 2018.

    The show chronicled Pretty Ricky’s return to the music scene and him and his brother’s personal life. 

    It also focuses on Blue’s family and his father helping to manage their career. 

    Pretty Ricky was mostly successful in the early 2000’s and took a hiatus in 2011. 

    The R&B group came back in 2019 with a Millenium Tour joining other popular artists from the early 2000’s like Chingy, Omarion, and Lloyd.

    In 2020, Pretty Ricky released a new single titled Body which was set to appear in the group’s new album.

    Was Baby Blue arrested?

    Blue was arrested in October of 2020 for allegedly pocketing millions of dollars in a coronavirus Paycheck Protection Program (PPP) loan scam.
