    Luis, who tied the knot with the Bravo star in August 2022, shared a photo of him kissing his wife on the cheek.

    "I got you [heart emoji] #bravocon20204," Luis wrote in the caption.

    He wore a black collared T-shirt with gray jeans while Teresa, 51, rocked a black jumpsuit with gold fringe on the bottom.

    "Teresa I wasn't asking you, you can [sit down]," she snapped.
