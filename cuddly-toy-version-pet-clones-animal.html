    NOBODY likes to be separated from their pet and saying goodbye can be the worst part of the day - especially for long periods of time.

    But now you can take a toy version of your beloved pet with you wherever you go.

    Cuddle Clones use a few photos of your pet to create an impressive replica– so you’ll never be separated from them again.

    You can even catch your pet’s personality, by choosing how you want their ears, tails, and mouth to look – so if you picture your pooch with perked up ears, and a grinning smile, your soft toy can have the same expression.

    Whether you have a dog, cat, horse, rabbit, pig, or guinea pig, Cuddle Clones can perfectly recreate your furry friend with

    All you need to do is send in at least one photo (although more is preferable, for a more accurate toy), and they’ll do the rest.

    The examples on the website are enough to have you wanting two (why not have one sleepy and one excited version?), and you can even have your pet made into slippers or golf head covers.

    Animal lovers will love the fact that the website donates some of their profits to animal charities from around the world.

    For smaller pets, prices start at £129.50, and larger animals begin at £144 – so you may need to start saving up, or dropping some hints as a gift idea.
