    After so many failed relationships, Ray J has finally found the love of his life. He and his wife, Princess Love have been experiencing the best of the days after they got married and had a baby. Want to know more about their wedding day or about their current love life with their precious child? Keep on reading! 

    Ray J and wife Princess Love: Married Life

    Ray J cried when Princess Love walked down the aisle

    Ray J and his princess got hitched on in front of around of close friends and family. You will be touched to know that Ray J broke into tears when Princess love walked down the aisle.

    While talking about why he cried, Ray J told ,

    Watching Princess walking down the aisle … when I heard the music and I saw her come down, I lost it. I was like, I’m just gonna let it go and enjoy the love and positive emotion. I got real happy as the tears dropped.

    Ray J feels lucky to have wife Princess Love in his life

    There were nine groomsmen including the rapper The Game who was among the crowds. Similarly, Ray J’s sister and famous R&B singer, Brandy served as one of Love’s bridesmaid. Brandy sang  for the first dance of the newlyweds.

    It seems like Ray J feels really blessed to have Princess in his life. He said to

    I’ve been learning how to listen and understand, and now we’re at this moment where we’re together and it’s official.

    Ecstatically, he further said,

    We both have the same last name. I’m so happy that everything worked out and Princess allowed me to come into her life and be the best man for her.

    Will Ray J and Princess Love get a divorce soon?

    However, post their big day, Ray J and his wife Princess Love had to go through various fan criticisms. Lots of them told that their wedding was fake and they did it only for publicity purposes. They even claimed that their wedding will not last long.

    Princess Love talks about their marriage

    However, Ray J and his wife said that they will be married for many years. During an interview with , Princess said:

    We changed our lifestyle a lot and made it more about us. I can see a difference in Ray and I know that he is going to be faithful and going, to be honest because he wouldn’t have asked me to marry him or take it to this step if he wasn’t ready.

    It seems like even Princess has planned to remain faithful towards her husband.

    Ray J is against his haters

    Even Ray J agreed with what his newlywed wife was saying. He said:

    We still gotta go through our ups and downs in a relationship but if we know that we’re going to do that together … everything is just beautiful. But it takes me first to step up as a man and just do what I gotta do, and No. 1 just be faithful. … Me and Princess are for sure going to make it. F--- the naysayers, f--- ‘em.
