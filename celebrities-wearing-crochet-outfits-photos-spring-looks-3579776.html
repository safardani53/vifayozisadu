    So many stars are all about crochet looks this spring season! Whether they’re fashioning skirts, bikini tops, crop tops or even dresses, celebrities have proven that crochet is a fun, versatile fabric. Crochet reminds so many of the fun, care-free feeling of springtime festivals, outdoor events, and more. With the season in full swing, and temperatures continuing to rise, we’re taking a look at some of the stars who have fashioned crochet looks with all of the confidence in the world!

    Shay Mitchell

    Shay Mitchell has always been on top of major trends. On April 13, 2019, the Pretty Little Liars alum, 34, was photographed on the arrival carpet at the Revolve Festival event at Coachella in La Quinta wearing her fabulous crochet look. Shay fashioned a black crochet dress from the brand atop her matching bodysuit. Shay belted the look to give it some fierce edge, and paired the ensemble with black ankle booties.

    Saweetie

    Saweetie also got the crochet memo when she attended the Revolve Festival event at Coachella on April 14, 2019. The beloved rapper, 27, wore a matching set of crochet shorts and a bikini top, featuring pastel hues of lavender, pink, and cream. She paired the outfit with a set of white booties for the fun, vintage ensemble.

    Nicole Scherzinger

    Nicole Scherzinger proved that crochet can be worn for any formal occasion! She attended Clive Davis’ 2019 Pre-Grammy Gala at The Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles on February 9, 2019 wearing this stunning black crochet dress. The singer and longtime entertainer, 42, wore the Julien Macdonald dress with total confidence, and showed off the garment’s keyhole back and incredible detailing running up her legs.

    Madison Pettis

    Madison Pettis proved that she was into the crochet trend when she attended the Revolve Festival event at Coachella on April 13, 2019. The actress, 22, fashioned this see-through, crochet skirt and bold black top for the event, looking super cute and stylish! Want to see more photos of stars rocking crochet styles?
