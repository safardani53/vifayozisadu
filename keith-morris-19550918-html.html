    Keith Morris is an American singer and songwriter best known for his role as the leader of heavyweight punk bands Black Flag, Circle Jerks and Off! Born and raised in Hermosa Beach, Calif., He founded Black Flag at the age of 21 with guitarist Greg Ginn and performed on Nervous Breakdown's 1979 EP. Shortly after leaving Black Flag in 1979, he founded the Circle Jerks with guitarist Greg Hetson; The group released seven albums between 1980 and 1995 and broke up and reformed several times. In 2009 Morris formed the super group Off! with guitarist Dimitri Coats, bassist Steven Shane McDonald and drummer Mario Rubalcaba. Morris has also appeared as a guest singer on a number of albums by other artists.
