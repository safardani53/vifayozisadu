    SCOTLAND rugby star Finn Russell is in action later today as his side face South Africa in their Rugby World Cup 2023 opener.

    Russell will be hoping his side can get off to the perfect start but they face a tough task against the Springboks.

    South Africa won the Rugby World Cup four years ago, beating England in the final.

    Scotland and Russell will be aiming to go as far as they possibly can, and supporting him all the way will be his doting girlfriend Emma Canning.

    Who is Finn Russell's girlfriend Emma Canning, do they have kids?

    Canning, 25, lives in Paris with Russell, who plays for French club Racing 92.

    Canning and Russell have been together for a number of years but breifly split in 2021 before getting back together.

    She has over 20,000 followers on Instagram, where he shares various snaps of her glamorous life with Russell.

    But before her life in France, Canning was a promising track and field star and competed as a heptahlete.

    Last year, Canning posted a picture of herself and Russell, but a troll commented: "Must be invigorating to know your only talent is to be in a relationship with a talented person."
