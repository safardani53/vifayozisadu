    Duck Dynastys Sadie Robertson &amp; husband Christian Huff expecting second child just one year after

    DUCK Dynasty star Sadie Robertson and her husband Christian Huff have announced some exciting baby news. Roughly one year after they welcomed their daughter, the couple is expecting their second child.

    07 Oct 2024, 00:00

    DUCK Dynasty star Sadie Robertson and her husband Christian Huff have announced some exciting baby news.

    Roughly one year after they welcomed their daughter, the couple is expecting their second child.

    The couple shared the exciting news on Instagram, posting a sweet photo of themselves and their daughter, snapped by photographer Jayda Iye.

    Sadie, 25, held a series of ultrasound photos in her hand while resting her head on her husband's shoulder.

    She captioned the post: "Another little miracle is in motion."

    Fans and friends flocked to the comments to wish the couple well and celebrate their happy news.

    More Duck Dynasty

    Audrey Roloff commented: "Awe congrats Sadie!!!!"

    Joy Dugger wrote: "How exciting! Congratulations you guys!"

    Family Friend and fellow Duck Dynasty alum Justin Martin, who recently welcomed twins with his wife, joked: "You're slacking if you ain't doing 2 at a time."

    Carlin Bates commented: "We are thrilled for your growing family."

    Most read in Entertainment

    NEW HOME

    The news comes several months after The U.S. Sun reported that Sadie and her husband bought a $20K plot of land in Louisiana.

    At the time, the home they had been living in was infested by rats.

    She revealed their rodent woes online.

    Saide wrote on Instagram: "Tbh the start to this year has been quite interesting! We will share more about it in our vlog soon.

    "It started off by me getting FLURONA (flu + covid) (yikes) so obviously I was so bummed to miss passion but I was so thankful that God gave me the strength to still preach my message to an empty room despite being so sick.

    "My faith from that experience grew so much. His spirit is truly living within us! also, thankful the conference is online!”

    Sadie added: "Then as we were on our way home from ATL we got a call that we have a couple of rats invading our home and those little guys are doing some workkk. So we couldn’t go home.

    "It’s day 4 of not being able to get them and being out of our house. It’s disgusting and we are exhausted from getting over sickness and being out of our home.

    "So here we are floating around trying to make the most of it. Thankful to be healthy now. Lord, help us to consider it joy! P.s. I’m sad to say ratatouille the movie hits different now.”

    Sadie's flurona diagnosis came after the TV star was hospitalized with Covid while she was pregnant with Honey in 2020.

    The couple purchased a 1.32-acre plot of land in June 2021.

    Records did not confirm at the time whether or not they broke ground on the property.

    In September, Sadie confirmed the foundation for the home they're building on that property was poured.

    The couple celebrated the exciting news with a sweet family photo, seemingly standing on that very foundation.

    Sadie gushed in her caption: "When Christian and I moved into our first rental home, on our first meal, we had a conversation about what we wanted our home to be. Not the way we wanted it to look, but what our home would mean to us and others. During that conversation, we said, “this house will set the tone.” whoever we are “out there” will first be created here. Who we are publicly will flow from who we are privately. 

    "Our house will be a house that fears the Lord, raises disciples, hosts well, loves deeply, worships naturally, dances frequently welcomes with grace, laughs often, and picks each other up when we fall!"

    She went on: "at 2:00 am the other night our foundation for our first home was laid and at 2 am that night Christian and I were on our knees praying for the solid foundation our home would stand on!

    "As we intentionally build physically it is so important that we build spiritually as well, on a solid foundation! Families can fall apart in the strongest of physical home, so we are laying our foundation on something STRONGER than concrete… the word of God! Underneath this concrete is a bible with Psalm 1 up! Can’t wait to make a million memories in our home and see our family grow (in the future) :)"
