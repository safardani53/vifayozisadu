    AMY Robach's replacement Eva Pilgrim has been joined by a returning Good Morning America host.

    Eva, 40, replaced Amy, 50, after she was fired from the show due to her on-air affair with another former anchor TJ Holmes at the beginning of the year.

    Around the same time, Alicia Quarles, 42, left GMA3 to start her maternity leave.

    On Monday, she came back to the daytime television show for the first time since giving birth to twins.

    To honor her return, she posted a heartfelt Instagram post with photos from her first day back.

    The first photo shows the host sitting on an orange bench that looks like a vintage TV with "GMA3" written above her.

    BABIES ON BOARD

    Back in February, Alicia gave birth to fraternal twins after having a terrifying health scare at a hospital.

    The TV personality recently revealed her babies' names in a new post she shared on social media.

    the media host posted snaps of her with her newborn children in a carousel post on Instagram.

    The images included in Alicia's photo dump showed the ABC anchor holding her pregnant belly in a purple two-piece outfit and cradling her twins following their birth.

    She wore a pink button-down shirt under a gray cardigan that said "Mama" in the photo where she held her kids in her arms as she looked at them lovingly.

    Another photo in the post featured Alicia in a pink robe, nuzzling her twin son and daughter to her bosom.

    The broadcast journalist had her daughter Harlow wrapped in a pink blanket and her son Hudson in a blue one in the photos.

    MIRACLE BIRTH

    Shortly after she gave birth, Alicia opened up to People about what happened while she delivered her kids.

    "My girl was breech and wouldn't turn...I had no idea about this recovery," she told the publication.

    People reported that though Alicia's twins are "100 percent healthy," they and Alicia will remain in the hospital for a "few more days."

    During her interview with People, she got candid about her road to motherhood and her "shocking" pregnancy.
