    KRIS Jenner showed off her glassware room that features a $770 tray and a $440 gold goblet.

    The 66-year-old gave fans a look at the expensive collection after she was slammed for flaunting her wealth.

    Kris’ oldest daughter Kourtney, 42, gave Keeping Up With the Kardashians fans a look into the momager’s impressive glassware room in a new post for her lifestyle brand Poosh.

    The collection- which is stored in a walk-in closet- is located right off of Kris’ kitchen.

    The room is fully stocked with goblets, flutes, coffee and tea trays, cocktail glasses and other essential hosting items.

    TRAY COLLECTION

    One silver tray features cup holders that ensures Kris’ cups will remain secure at all times.

    Another silver tray holds silver coffee pots and tea kettles with black handles, while a tiny silver cup holds toothpicks.

    Kris will never run out of trays, as another rectangular silver tray holds even more silver tea kettles and coffee pots with wooden handles.

    An additional tray that Kris uses is the Vertigo Tray With Handles by Christofle, which retails for a whopping $770.

    The photo tour continued with a shot of Kris' many drinking glasses, which was placed in glass cabinets.

    DRINKING GLASSES

    The Kardashian matriarch has plenty of drinking options and her collection includes cocktail glasses, wine glasses, goblets and more.

    Many of the expensive cups are made out of glass and feature gold detailings.

    A number of the glasses are from a Moser collection- including the Splendid Gold Goblet 340 Ml that costs $474 and the Splendid Gold Brandy Snifter 320ml that retails for $337.00.

    Other items featured in the collection include glass wine glasses with thick gold stems and a variety of gold cups.

    KITCHEN TOUR

    In addition to her glassware collection, Kris has also given fans a look into her luxury dish room and the kitchen inside of her luxurious California mansion.

    She was recently slammed as "wasteful" for having two massive fridges filled with fruits, vegetables, eggs and more.

    In a recent issue of Poosh, Kris opened her kitchen doors and gave fans a glimpse of her abundant appliances.

    According to the site, Kris has two refrigerators, "of which the middle one is the showstopper with fresh fruit, vegetables, and herbs all perfectly organized and on display."

    Another fridge holds drinks, condiments, and more, while her massive freezer is "always equipped with her favorite selection of ice cream."

    FLAUNTING HER WEALTH

    Kourtney showed off Kris' collection after the momager was ripped for flaunting her wealth.

    Just months ago, Kris was spotted with a Hermès Black Crocodile Birkin Bag that retails up to $100K.
