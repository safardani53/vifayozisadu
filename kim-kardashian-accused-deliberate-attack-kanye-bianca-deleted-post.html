    DAD DUTY

    Earlier this week, Kanye and Bianca were on kid duty as the Yeezy architect was photographed carrying Chicago, five in Miami, Florida.

    North, 10, and Saint, eight, were also spotted in photos - but not Kim and Kanye's youngest, Psalm, four.

    The family attended Kanye's launch party for his new album, Vultures.

    Bianca wore an oversized leather trench coat teamed with red leather boots and a navy headscarf.

    She held tightly onto Chicago - who was wearing a denim jacket and jeans - on her hip as they walked through Wynwood Marketplace.

    The Flashing Lights rapper walked alongside them, wearing a black T-shirt and pants with a black sweater tied over his face.

    Saint was close by in a gray sweater and soccer shorts, while North wore a gray T-shirt and leather jacket as she held hands with a female companion.

    The family was celebrating Kanye's comeback album Vultures, which The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed he will launch on December 15.

    HIS BIG COMEBACK

    Despite being canceled last year and losing countless fashion deals following his anti-Semitic remarks, his music career does not appear to have taken a hit.

    He has been in Miami all weekend with Bianca and a huge entourage, previewing tracks from the new record at multiple events.

    The rap music star even took to the stage at DJ Khaled's birthday party at Club LIV to perform tracks from his back catalog, including All of the Lights, Can't Tell Me Nothing, Runaway, and Jesus Walks.

    Several witnesses in the crowd took to X and Instagram to share videos, revealing that he gave fans info on his project.

    "It's called Vultures, coming out this Friday."

    Along with Ty Dolla $ign, it will feature A-list celebs including Nicki Minaj and Young Thug, according to sources, with a full track list already appearing on social media leak accounts.

    Even North has her own verse in a song - which she debuted live on stage.
