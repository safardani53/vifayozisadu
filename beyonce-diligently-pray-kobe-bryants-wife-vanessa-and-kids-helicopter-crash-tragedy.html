    BEYONCE has shared a heartfelt tribute to Kobe Bryant and his daughter Gianna following their tragic deaths.

    The singer, 38, has revealed she will "continue to diligently pray" for the Bryant family as she took to social media to offer her condolences.

    Beyonce paid tribute to the the late basketball star and his 13-year-old daughter, who lost their lives in a helicopter accident, by posting a single photo of them both to her Instagram page.

    The snap of Kobe was taken from when he was young.

    She later shared a photo of them together.

    The mom of three wrote: "I will continue to diligently pray for your Queens. You are deeply missed beloved Kobe."

    Beyonce and Kobe had been friends for many years, with the singer often a regular guest at Los Angeles Lakers games.

    The sports world was left reeling on Sunday, as news filtered in that Bryant was killed in a helicopter accident.

    The chopper came down after getting into trouble flying in thick fog.

    He is survived by wife Vanessa and his other daughters Natalia, Bianca and Capri, who was born in June 2019.

    In her first public post since the accident, Vanessa, 37, said she was uncertain "what our lives will hold."

    She said: "I’m not sure what our lives hold beyond today, and it’s impossible to imagine life without them.

    "But we wake up each day, trying to keep pushing because Kobe, and our baby girl, Gigi, are shining on us to light the way.

    "Our love for them is endless — and that’s to say, immeasurable. I just wish I could hug them, kiss them and bless them. Have them here with us, forever."
