    What time does racing start at Brighton?

    The first race is at 2.20pm.

    There are seven races on the card and the last race is due off at 5.25pm.

    What TV channel is Brighton on?

    You can watch the racing at Brighton on At The Races.

    Attheraces is on Sky channel 415, Virgin channel 534, and UPC in Ireland on channel 418.

    What is the going at the racecourse?

    The going is currently Good to Soft, Good in places.
