    Though she kept her wedding ceremony a secret, Mean Girls actress Lacey Chabert recently shared some “grool” photos from her big day on Twitter, Instagram, and her new blog. 

    The 31-year-old got married in Los Angeles to Dave Nehdar, and on Jan. 4, she tweeted, “I’m starting off 2014 as a Mrs! Over the holidays, my best friend & love of my life…we became husband & wife! #soblessed #love.”

    The bride wore at least two dresses for her big day. For the ceremony, Chabert went with a tradition strapless A-line dress with a sweetheart neckline, and for the reception, she slipped into a sexier strapless mermaid gown with a bejeweled headband. 

    Several of her celebrity friends, including fellow newlywed Big Bang Theory actress Kaley Cuoco andBachelorette alum Ali Fedotowsky attended the nuptials. 

    On her blog, the Telling of the Shoes actress talks about how special it was to have her father walk her down the aisle.
