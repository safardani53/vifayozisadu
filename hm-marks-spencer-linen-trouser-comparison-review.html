    A FASHION fan has revealed that they bought two pairs of almost identical trousers from H&M and Marks & Spencer and settled on one clear winner. 

    The stylish social media user said they were keen to try out this year’s summer trend – linen trousers – and realised that both H&M and Marks & Spencer were selling very similar ones.

    What’s more, the fashion items, which come in a variety of colours, are both the exact same price at £25.

    So TikToker @ighyvx modelled them both and then gave her honest review and verdict.

    She uploaded a video to the social media platform and encouraged others to run to M&S as they were the runaway winner.

    Alongside footage of her unpackaging a pair of white and black linen trousers, she said: “Your sign to ditch H&M and buy M&S pants.”
