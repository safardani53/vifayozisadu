    Tyler Baoltierra and Catelynn Baltierra have been in the spotlight for quite some time now.

    They are famous for appearing on the hit MTV show Teen Mom, but there’s more to this couple than just reality TV drama.

    Let’s take a closer look at their net worth, married life, and some interesting tidbits about them.

    Tyler and Catelynn Baltierra’s net worth revealed

    Tyler has quite a hefty bank account, with a net worth of $5 million.

    Catelynn’s income is primarily from her career as a reality television personality, author, and public speaker. She became a household name through Teen Mom, a spin-off of the MTV show 16 and Pregnant.

    Reports suggest that Catelynn earned a whopping $500,000 per season of Teen Mom OG.

    Tyler, on the other hand, is an entrepreneur and an aspiring artist. He’s tried his hand at various business endeavors, which have undoubtedly contributed to his impressive net worth.

    Tyler Baltierra and Catelynn Baltierra’s income sources

    Apart from their TV careers, Tyler and Catelynn have dabbled in entrepreneurship. In March 2015, they co-authored a book titled Conquering Chaos.

    This book delves into their struggles and how they managed to overcome them. It’s an inspiring read for anyone facing challenges in life.

    But that’s not all. After the birth of their daughter, Nova, in 2015, the couple launched their own children’s clothing brand called Tierra Reign.

    This brand focuses on producing high-quality children’s clothing, and the income from it has been a significant contributor to their net worth.

    Tyler Baltierra’s smoking hot selfie! (Source: Instagram)

    Catelynn even encouraged Tyler to join the adult social media platform OnlyFans.

    The couple took their fans by surprise by launching an OnlyFans account to celebrate Tyler’s impressive weight loss transformation. This new income source is expected to boost their net worth even further.

    Fans had been clamoring for Tyler to join OnlyFans for a while, and Catelynn enthusiastically announced the news on her Instagram story.

    She even shared a link to Tyler’s OnlyFans page, where he proudly displayed his muscles. With this new venture, fans can expect exclusive content from the reality TV star on OnlyFans.

    Tyler and Catelynn Baltierra’s financial ups and downs

    While the couple has certainly seen their bank accounts grow, they’ve also faced their fair share of financial challenges.

    In 2020, it was reported that Catelynn and Tyler owed the government over $800,000 in back taxes.

    However, Tyler managed to sell their Burtchville, Michigan home for $175,000 in October 2020, making a substantial profit.

    He had initially listed the house for $169,900 in June 2020, so that’s a profit of $101,560.

    The house itself had three bedrooms, two bathrooms, a kitchen with granite countertops, stainless steel appliances, and more.

    It was a lovely place, but the couple decided to move on to a historic four-bedroom, two-bathroom Octagon-shaped home they purchased in May 2017 for $220,000.

    Sneak peek into Tyler and Catelynn Baltierra’s married life

    Tyler and Catelynn have a love story that began in middle school. They started dating in 2005 and have been through a lot together.

    Their journey to parenthood was documented on MTV’s 16 and Pregnant in 2009 when Catelynn gave birth to their daughter Carly.

    It was a tough decision, but they placed Carly up for adoption. Despite the hardship, they’ve remained bonded throughout the years.

    Tyler proposed to Catelynn twice, first in high school in 2010 and again in 2013, after a brief breakup. Two years later, they welcomed their second child, Nova, in January 2015. Seven months later, they tied the knot after dating on and off for a decade.

    Their family grew when they welcomed their daughter Vaeda in February 2019 after experiencing a miscarriage. This rainbow baby brought joy after Catelynn publicly discussed her battle with mental illness and sought treatment.

    In February 2021, the couple announced they were expecting again after another miscarriage the year before. Their daughter, Rya Rose, was born in August of the same year.

    Currently, the gorgeous couple is enjoying a blissful married life as they continue to raise their growing family.

    Know Tyler Baltierra better

    Tyler was born on January 12, 1992, in Clemens, Michigan, USA. He is 31 years old as of 2023. His parents are Kimberly Forbes and Butch Baltierra.

    Talking about his siblings, he has two siblings, Amber Baltierra and Sik World.

    As for his education, Tyler attended university and graduated with a focus on baking. He is now an aspiring musical artist and a reality TV personality.

    You can now listen to his recently released single, ‘Anybody’ on Spotify, YouTube, TIDAL, and Amazon Music.

    Tyler Baltierra on ‘Teen Mom: The Next Chapter’

    The couple’s journey continues on the MTV show Teen Mom: The Next Chapter. This series premiered on September 6, 2022. It’s a follow-up to their previous appearances on Teen Mom OG and 16 and Pregnant.

    Fans have witnessed the couple’s ups and downs, but they’re still going strong in 2023.

    Catelynn teased that viewers can expect to see Tyler working through some of his internal trauma with ketamine therapy. And if the show gets picked up for another season, she plans to come into her own as a person.

    After all, Tyler and Catelynn have come a long way since their days on 16 and Pregnant. Their net worth has grown significantly thanks to their TV careers, entrepreneurship ventures, and their recent foray into OnlyFans.

    Their journey from middle school sweethearts to married MTV stars and their commitment to making their relationship work is really inspiring.

    Despite the financial ups and downs, they continue to face life’s challenges together, and their story is far from over as they continue to share it on Teen Mom: The Next Chapter.
