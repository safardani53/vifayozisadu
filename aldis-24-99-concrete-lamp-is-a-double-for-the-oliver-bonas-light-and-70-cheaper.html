    CONCRETE is the cool, clean, yet obscenely expensive homeware trend that is all over our Instagram interior feeds right now.

    Oozing Scandi style and sophistication, Aldi has come to the rescue again with an on-trend concrete lamp that costs just £24.99.

    • Concrete-effect lamp, £24.99 from Aldi - buy now

    A decent bargain which can simply be thrown in with your weekly shop, this concrete-effect lamp is an astonishingly convincing dupe for its Oliver Bonas counterpart.

    While Oliver Bonas’ stylish offering comes in at £95 (not including a bulb), Aldi’s bargain buy is just a quarter of this price and is good to go from the moment you get it home.

    Perfect for your bedside table or a stylish way to update your living space, the concrete base is a subtle nod to effortlessly minimal Scandinavian interiors.

    An added bonus of its stylish, hefty concrete base makes it a tough task for children, pets, and/or husbands to accidentally knock over.

    The lamp’s golden neck will accentuate any other metal features you may have about your home and looks stunningly chic when placed next to pastels.

    While this homeware steal is undoubtedly a trend-led piece, the concrete lamp makes for an understated addition to the home which can be repurposed and experimented with as your personal taste evolves.

    Make sure to combine with plenty of scented candles, cosy blankets, and a lit fireplace for a full-on Hyggae effect.
