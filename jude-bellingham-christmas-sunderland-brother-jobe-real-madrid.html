    JUDE BELLINGHAM has shared a host of Christmas pictures as his family reunited in Sunderland.

    Jude, 20, and younger brother Jobe, 18, both secured summer transfers to Real Madrid and the Black Cats respectively.

    And Bellingham family split up, with Denise living with Jude in Spain, and dad Mark in the North East with Jobe.

    However, thanks to LaLiga's two-week winter break, they could all spend the festive season together.

    Jude took the chance to catch his brother in action in the Championship as he attended Jobe's two Sunderland games either side of Christmas.

    He watched the Black Cats lose 3-0 at home to Coventry, before they secured a 1-0 win away at Hull.
