    JUST because the racing has finished for the day doesn't mean the fun stops at Cheltenham.

    It's all about getting together with friends and having a good time.

    Hopefully you'll be splashing some winnings - if not then no doubt recalling how your good thing got nutted on the line in the lucky last.

    Sun Racing has scouted out the best drinking spots to go at the end of each day.

    Drink responsibly, have a good time and bash those bookies.

    1 The Retreat

    Line your stomach for the day with one of their hearty breakfasts or catch up for a drink later in the evening.

    Latest Cheltenham Offers

    The Retreat has always proved a popular spot with punters.

    And if you can't make it to the track but just want to sample the atmosphere of the town during the week, they show the races on TV.

    Address: 10/11 Suffolk Parade, Cheltenham GL50 2AB

    2 The Feathered Fish

    Guest ale, classic British grub and a huge, fun atmosphere.

    Address: 104-106 Winchcombe St, Cheltenham, GL52 2NW

    3 The Beehive

    Buzzing atmosphere - pardon the pun - and full of racing fanatics, the Beehive is a great spot on any night.

    This has been rated one of the top six real ale pubs in Cheltenham so you're bound to find a tasty tipple or two.

    Address: 1-3 Montpellier Villas, Cheltenham GL50 2XE

    4 The Bottle Of Sauce

    'Outrageously' delicious drinks are on offer in this rustic-chic boozer.

    In the heart of Cheltenham, it's famed for its live music and pizzas.

    Address: Ambrose St, Cheltenham GL50 3LH

    5 The Tavern

    Delicious food, loads of drinks and a party atmosphere.
