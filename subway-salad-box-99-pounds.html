    I almost bought Subway's new brie salad box... then I saw it was 99

    11 Feb 2024, 00:00

    A SUBWAY fan was left baffled when he spotted the fast-food giant offering a salad for £99.

    Nick Krinthor was just about to order the chain’s new brie salad box through Just Eat when he noticed the price tag.

    He realised that, with delivery, the 194-calorie lunch would cost him more than £100.

    Other salads were listed at between £5 and £7.

    The brie salad box was listed for sale from its branch in Wythenshawe, Gtr Manchester, for five days before being marked as unavailable.

    Nick, 40, from Baguley, Greater Manchester, said: “It’s got to be the most expensive salad in the UK.

    "I almost added it to the basket before I realised the price.

    “I know the cost of living is going up but that’s taking liberties.”

    Subway declined to comment.
