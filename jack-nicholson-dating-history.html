    JACK Nicholson has dated his fair share of Hollywood's leading ladies.

    Previously, the Oscar-winning actor had relationships with Anjelica Huston and Lara Flynn Boyle.

    Who has Jack Nicholson dated?

    Following his divorce from his The Terror co-star Sandra Knight, Jack Nicholson went on to date:

    • Michelle Phillips
    • Anjelica Huston
    • Rebecca Broussard
    • Lara Flynn Boyle

    One of Jack's first romances in the public eye was with Michelle Phillips, the two dated between 1971 and 1972.

    After the end of his courtship with Michelle, the A Few Good Men actor moved on with Anjelica Huston.

    Read More on Jack Nicholson

    Jack and Anjelica were involved in an on-again, off-again relationship for 17 years.

    The two began dating sometime in 1973; they met at a house party hosted by Jack.

    Why did Jack and Anjelica split?

    In 1990, the Oscar-winning pair called it quits one final time.

    Their split was attributed to Jack's affair with his The Two Jakes co-star, Rebecca Broussard.

    In her 2013 memoir Watch Me, Anjelica remembers she was the one to end things with Jack.

    "There’s only room for one of us women in the picture, and I am going to retire from it," The Addams Family actress recalled.

    She further explained that the end of her and Jack's relationship led to one final confrontation between them.

    Upon learning he had cheated on her with a third woman via a Playboy article, Anjelica set out to confront him one more time.

    When she located him at his Paramount Pictures office, she ambushed the One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest star.

    "He was coming out of the bathroom when I attacked him," she confessed.

    "I don’t think I kicked him but I beat him savagely about the head and shoulders.

    "He was ducking and bending, and I was going at him like a prizefighter, raining a vast array of direct punches.

    "I felt a strange underlying gratitude to him for allowing me to beat the living hell out of him."

    Following their breakup, Jack moved on with Rebecca Broussard and Anjelica married Robert Graham.

    When did Jack date Lara Flynn Boyle?

    Jack and Rebecca split in 1994 and five years later, he began dating Twin Peaks actress Lara Flynn Boyle.

    From 1999 to 2004, Jack and Lara were on-again, off-again.

    Ultimately, things did not work out between them.

    The two stars called it off for good in 2004 and Lara went on to marry her second husband Donald Ray Thomas II.

    When was Jack married?

    From 1962 to 1968, Jack was married to Sandra Knight.

    Sandra is also the mother of Jack's first child Jennifer; she was born on September 13, 1963.

    Although his marriage to Sandra remains his only one, his ex-wife married once more to John Arthur Stephenson.

    In addition to Jennifer, Jack has an additional five children.

    He also has two daughters named Honey and Tessa, and an older son named Caleb.
