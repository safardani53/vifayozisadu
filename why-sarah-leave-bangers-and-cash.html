    CAR enthusiast Sarah Crabtree was a cast member on the docuseries Bangers and Mash from 2019 until 2021.

    But what happened to her after the show and what is she doing now? find out here.

    Who is Sarah from Bangers and Cash?

    Sarah Crabtree is a well-known British TV star and a businesswoman.

    As per LinkedIn, she graduated from Wolfreton School in 1986.

    She started working in 2009 and her first-ever job was as a manager at The Chocolate Factory to which she stayed at for seven years.

    She joined Mathewsons Classic Cars in 2016.

    She remained with the car-auctioning business for four years.

    Sarah rose to fame after she featured in the Yesterday's show, Bangers and Cash.

    She appeared on the series until the fourth season, which was also the final one.

    Sarah is on Instagram with over eight thousand followers as of September 2022.

    Search for her account under the username @sarah_crabtreee.

    Why did Sarah leave Bangers and Cash?

    Sarah left the Bangers and Cash show in May 2021.

    She captioned an Instagram post at this time saying: “Series 4 of ‘Bangers’ has come to an end which means no more Crabbers on the tellybox“.

    Following the end of the show, Sarah served as an office manager for the Mathewsons Classic Cars.

    She remained with the car-auctioning business for four years.

    latest on showbiz

    What does Sarah do now?

    As of 2022, Sarah has remained in the classic car business.

    She began her own business venture and started her company, Evoke Classics.

    Evoke Classic is a platform in which classic car enthusiasts can buy and sell classic vehicles.

    As per Sarah’s LinkedIn page, the company was due to launch in October 2021 and outlined their mission as follows: “…to make available a range of historically significant motor cars, all the best example of type you will find for the given model and price.“

    She has a team that works with her at Evoke and as her bio on the official Evoke website states, she’s “friendly, trustworthy and approachable“.
