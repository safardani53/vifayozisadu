    SAVANNAH Chrisley’s ex-fiancé Nic Kerdiles is fueling those reconciliation rumors as he works out with her brother Grayson at the gym. 

    The ex-professional hockey player looked ripped as he completed a set of bicep curls before showing 14-year-old Grayson on doing leg stretches. 

    Posting the videos to his Instagram Stories, the 26-year-old appeared in good spirits as he shared a snippet of his work out with fans. 

    The sighting of Nic with Savannah's little brother is sure to add fuel to the fire of those reconciliation rumors after the pair ended their engagement in early 2020.

    Fans of the former Chrisley Knows Best couple started asking the sportsman if he and Savannah had rekindled their relationship after he posted a photo on Instagram with her dad and brother. 

    "Glad you're still there with the family," one fan commented as another asked: "So are you and Savannah official again?"

    The picture showed Nic with patriarch Todd and his son Chase, whom Savannah lives with in LA.

    Nic met Savannah, 23, in 2017 and the pair enjoyed a long-distance romance as he played hockey in Canada.

    He proposed in 2019 but after the pair moved in together they realized they had moved too fast, and put the wedding and their romance on hold.

    The reality TV star revealed that she and her now ex-fiancé are in therapy weeks after Savannah's decision to say no to getting married to avoid the "divorce culture."

    The 23-year-old opened up to ET about her choice and her plans moving forward, explaining that she and Nic are taking time to focus on themselves.

    She said: "You’re allowed to have those boundaries and you’re allowed to figure it out for yourselves.

    "And you don’t have to make someone else comfortable while making yourself uncomfortable."

    She added: "That’s kind of the place we’re in.
