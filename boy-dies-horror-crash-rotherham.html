    A SCHOOLBOY pedestrian has tragically died after being hit by two cars.

    The 14-year-old was walking in Rotherham when the horror unfolded at around 6.23pm yesterday.

    Emergency crews were scrambled to the scene but sadly he couldn't be saved and was declared dead at the scene.

    South Yorkshire Police confirmed both drivers raised the alarm and are assisting officers with their enquiries.

    The force is now appealing for witnesses to come forward following the tragedy.
