    Floyd Schofield, aka Kid Austin, born on August 27, 2002, is a professional super featherweight boxer. He is recognized mainly for his orthodox right-handed boxing style.

    The WBA International Champion comes from a family of boxers, as his grandfather and father were professional boxers. He began boxing at the early age of three.

    The emerging boxing prodigy stepped into his first amateur fight at the tender age of seven, marking the start of his journey in the ring. 

    ABF Lightweight Continental Champion made his professional boxing debut on October 9, 2020, defeating Richard Esquibel via first-round knockout.

    Since then, Floyd has rapidly built out his undefeated professional record, claiming multiple knockout wins.
