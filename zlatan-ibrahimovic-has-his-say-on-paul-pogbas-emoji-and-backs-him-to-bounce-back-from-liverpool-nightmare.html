    Zlatan Ibrahimovic has his say on Paul Pogba's emoji... and backs him to bounce back from Liverpool

    ZLATAN IBRAHIMOVIC says Paul Pogba can handle the heat at Old Trafford. There was plenty of flak flying after the worlds most expensive player gave away a penalty and failed to impress in Manchester Uniteds 1-1 draw with Liverpool.

    05 May 2024, 00:00

    ZLATAN IBRAHIMOVIC says Paul Pogba can handle the heat at Old Trafford.

    There was plenty of flak flying after the world’s most expensive player gave away a penalty and failed to impress in Manchester United’s 1-1 draw with Liverpool.

    The £89million midfielder, 23, promoted his own emoji before the game and then turned up with a green streak in his hair.

    But Ibrahimovic, who netted Sunday’s equaliser, said: “Pressure is something I enjoy and I think he likes it also.

    “If you want to play at the top, pressure is 24 hours. It’s something we have to handle because we belong to the top — Paul belongs at the top.”

    As for this emoji, which was flashed on United’s own website on Friday, Ibra, 35, said: “When I started to play football there was no social media. But it’s part of the game now.

    Related stories

    “What is too much? We don’t know. We just follow the game.”

    Ibrahimovic hit his 19th goal of the season on Sunday but the Swede is not resting on his laurels.

    He said: “The pressure I have around me is nothing compared to the pressure I put on myself.

    “I really want to be more than perfect every game, even in training.

    “I’m not happy if I don’t win in training so imagine what it’s like in games.”

    Ibrahimovic is urging United boss Jose Mourinho to fight Real Madrid to sign the new HIM.

    Swedish whizkid Alexander Isak, 17, has been linked with a move to Spain from AIK Stockholm.

    But Ibra believes he could help persuade Isak to choose Old Trafford over the Bernabeu.
