    JANE Seymour is an English actress who rose to fame as a Bond Girl.

    Here's the full lowdown on the Playboy model who's showing no signs of slowing down and growing more glamorous by the year...

    Who is Jane Seymour?

    Jane Seymour, 68, is a British actress from London.

    Her mother Joyce was a nurse and her father Benjamin was a distinguished gynaecologist.

    Her first major film role came in 1970 when she played a Jewish woman fleeing Nazi persecution in The Only Way.

    In addition to acting she has also written several self-help books including Two At A Time: Having Twins and Among Angels.

    When did Jane Seymour pose for Playboy?

    Jane revealed she'd posed for Playboy magazine in February 2018, sharing a series of shots on her Instagram.

    Aged 67, she became the oldest women to pose for Playboy and the photoshoot marked her third time appearing in the magazine - previously featuring in the July 1973 and January 1987 issues.

    Jane insisted she felt sexier at 67 than she did in her younger years, explaining: "There's an enormous freedom in having lived as long as I have.

    "Like my father used to say, I'm comfortable in my own skin."

    What are her most famous roles?

    Jane starred as Bond girl Solitaire in the James Bond film Live and Let Die in 1973.
    She was cast as Elise McKenna in the 1980 movie Somewhere in Time.

    In 1981, she appeared in the television film East of Eden as Cathy Ames and her portrayal won her a Golden Globe.

    In 1983, she appeared nude in the film Lassiter with Tom Selleck.

    In the 90s, she earned critical praise for her role as Dr. Michaela Quinn in the television series Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman.

    She returned to the big screen in 2005 for comedy Wedding Crashers, starring alongside Owen Wilson and Vince Vaughn.

    Is Jane Seymour married?

    Jane Seymour has been married four times.

    She was with first husband, Richard Attenborough's son Michael, from 1971 to 1973.

    Her second marriage to his friend Geoffrey Platter lasted from 1977 to 1978.

    She was married to third husband David Flynn from 1981 to 1992 and they had daughter Katherine, 36, and son Sean, 32.

    In 1993 she married actor Jason Keach in 1993 and they had twins John Stacy and Kristopher Steven, 22.

    They divorced in December 2015.
