    Stunned Asda shopper finds a Little Mermaid swimsuit for sale with a TOPLESS Ariel on the front

    STUNNED shoppers were shocked to find a topless Disney princess on a child's swimsuit in Asda. Ariel, one of the favourites for Disney fans, was seen without her trusty shell bra due to a suspected manufacturing issue.

    03 Jul 2024, 00:00

    STUNNED shoppers were shocked to find a topless Disney princess on a child's swimsuit in Asda.

    Ariel, one of the favourites for Disney fans, was seen without her trusty shell bra due to a suspected manufacturing issue.

    The star of The Little Mermaid was left in a state of nudity as the swimming costumes were put on the shelves of the store at the Ventura Retail Park, Tamworth, missing a key item of the design.

    One shopper, Chelsea Mosson, 22, a sports development coach, said she was "mortified" after spotting the swimsuit, for Asda's George range.

    She wrote on Twitter: "Absolutely mortified to see this on Asda's shelf this evening - a 3-4 year old swimming costume. What's going on?"

    She said: "Me and my partner were quite shocked about it - it's a very bizarre one indeed."

    MOST READ IN NEWS

    Ariel fan Rachel Greaves, 36, said she grew up watching the film and was "shocked" that Asda had put the swimsuit on the shelves without checking it properly.

    Miss Greaves, of St Albans, Herts, said: "I couldn't believe it when I saw Ariel without her shells on - Asda staff should have spotted this as soon as the swimsuit was made.

    "God only knows how it slipped through quality control, but Ariel topless is certainly not how I want to think of a film I grew up with."

    An Asda spokesperson said: “We’re sorry for the printing error on the Little Mermaid swimsuit, which was caused during the production process.

    "It only affected a very small number of the swimsuits which were available from George, but we’d like to reassure our customers that we have removed all stock from sale and will ensure this doesn’t happen again.”
