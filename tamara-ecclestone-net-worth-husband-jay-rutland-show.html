    HEIRESS and model Tamara Ecclestone has been in the spotlight from an early age thanks to her father's fame.

    Tamara has worked for Formula One as well as launching her own TV shows, here's everything you need to know.

    Who is Tamara Ecclestone?

    Tamara Ecclestone is a British socialite and mum-of-one, married to Jay Rutland.

    She's the daughter of former F1 Chief Executive Bernie Ecclestone and model Slavica Radic.

    Her glamorous life began when she was born in Milan, Italy on June 28, 1984.

    The socialite was educated in London girls school Francis Holland and speaks English, Italian and Croatian.

    She has worked on a number of glamour and modelling shoots, as well as having her own reality shows.

    Tamara Ecclestone: Billion $$$ Girl aired on Channel 5 in 2011 then six years later she premiered Tamara's World on ITVBe.

    As well as modelling, she has turned her hand to telly presenting and hosted Sky Sports Italia's coverage of the 2009 Formula 1 season.

    She also presented the Red Bull Air Race World Championship for Channel 4 in 2006.

    Who is Tamara Ecclestone's husband Jay Rutland?

    Jay Rutland, born March 11, 1981, is a businessman from Essex.

    Together the pair have two daughters - Sophia, eight, and Serena, 18-months.

    Jay makes his money through his company Bigrate Business Developments, Ltd which operates with management consulting services.

    The pair married in 2013 and had Sophia the following year.

    Three years later, Jay came under public scrutiny after being accused of assisting drugs baron James Tarrant flee abroad.
