    KYLIE Jenner slammed a troll who called her Power Rangers costume "Plastic Rangers."

    The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star clapped back at the hater by saying "we love to recycle."

    On Thursday, the 23-year-old posted a video of her and her friends dressed up in Power Rangers costumes.

    The group posed for the camera and it panned to show the girls in their outfits.

    Kylie captioned the clip: "go go power rangers."

    While many of Kylie's fans applauded the group costume, one troll wrote: "SOMEONE SAID PLASTIC RANGERS PLEASE."

    The negative comment got Kylie's attention and she clapped back by simply writing: "and we love recycling."

    The KUWTK star also shared a photo of her and her friends dressed up as the Power Rangers on Friday.

    Kylie, who wore the Red Power Ranger suit, captioned the snap: "It's morphin' time."

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder was joined by Stassie Karanikolaou, Victoria Villarroel, Sofia Villarroel and Carter Gregory for the costume that acted as a tribute to the classic 90s show.

    Both Kylie and her older sister Kim Kardashian, 40, showed off their Halloween costumes on Thursday night.

    Similar to Kylie, Kim also opted to match her costume with a group.

    While the 40-year-old dressed up as Tiger King's Carole Baskin, her best friend Jonathan Cheban, 46, rocked a Joe Exotic costume.

    The KKW Beauty founder wore a leopard print top and jeans, while she accessorized the look with a long blonde wig, a purple flower crown and a full face of makeup.

    Kim's four children- who she shares with husband Kanye West- also got in on the fun and dressed up as tigers.

    North, seven, Saint, four, Chicago, two, and one-year-old Psalm looked adorable as they wore face paint and tiger onesies.

    Kim, Jonathan and the kids got into character as the group later posed for a photo shoot alongside a metal cage.
