    ‘Duck Dynasty’ Star Justin Martin Engaged — Congrats!

    Major celebrations are about to go down in West Monroe, Louisiana, because there’s about to be a wedding — cast member Justin Martin is engaged! The Duck Dynasty star who works at the Robertson’s Duck Commander business popped the question to his longtime girlfriend Brittany Brugman.

    The bride-to-be announced the exciting news on her Twitter by posting a photo of her new gorgeous accessory — a sparkling, diamond ring!

    He liked it so he put a ring on it 😜 I can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with @friar_martin_dc ! #BestBirthday pic.twitter.com/jnxQraUCRn

    — Brittany Brugman (@BrittanyBrugman) May 1, 2014

    She also included the hashtag “Best Birthday.” Talk about an amazing birthday present! Just the day before, Brittany tweeted about her proposal, Justin took to Twitter to wish his now fiancé a happy birthday. “Happy Birthday to the love of my life…Can’t wait to spend many more with you!”

    Justin, who is best known by his last name, Martin, retweeted Brittany’s big announcement to his 454,000 followers, and all of the couple’s fans are commenting with congratulatory notes. So sweet!

    Who Is Justin Martin?

    Justin first met the Robertson family 10 years ago at church, and he told A&E (the network that airs Duck Dynasty) that he didn’t have a beard before he knew them!

    “When I met them I had no beard because at our high school you could not have one if you were on the football team,” Justin says. “Oh, how things change!”

    He also recalls his admiration for the family, which began long before he worked at Duck Commander. “I grew up watching these guys duck hunt for a living,” he says. “I really looked up to them for their hunting skills and their faith.”

    But sweetest of all, the newly engaged bearded man swoons over his lady! On the network’s website, Justin describes himself very simply: “My first name is Justin. I’m only 27 years old. And I am in fact in a relationship with a great woman named Brittany.”

    Congrats!
