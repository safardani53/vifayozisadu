    A WOMAN was left in hysterics when she saw the "savage" names of plus-size stores in Asia.

    Erika took to TikTok to share a video of the store names, writing over the top: "Asian plus size stores be like..." 

    She began with "BeeBeeFat", "Fat Girls" and "Fatty Fatgirl".

    Next up was "Love Calories", "Moo Moo" and "Thai Fat" - which also had the tag "Beauty isn't a size".

    Erika concluded her rundown with "Fat Cat".

    And she captioned her video: "There was no need to be this savage."

    People were quick to comment on the video, with many taking issue with Love Calories.

    "LOVE CALORIES IS RUUUUDDDE," one wrote.

    "Love Calories had me -yiikkkeeesss," another added.

    While a third commented: "Love Calories? I’m offended but it’s also so funny."

    Others insisted the stores should reconsider their names, with one writing: "There’s a huge difference of being truthful and hurtful."

    "The names of these stores are the meaning of passive aggressive," another added.

    "LMAO this should be illegal," someone else commented.

    "They are unhinged fr," another raged.

    And some people used the comments section to reveal their own experiences in Asia.

    "When I was in Vietnam I walked into a clothing store and and the lady said ‘Wow so big’," one wrote.

    As another commented: "Haha when I was in Korea - I would be pushed out of stores by the attendants while they were saying ’NO BIG SIZE!’"
