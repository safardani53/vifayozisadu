    ABC News correspondent Linsey Davis has been married for over seven years. Judging by the lovely photos she shares on Instagram, the couple is living a blissful life. 

    On February 15, 2021, the Prime Live anchor shared a lovely picture with her husband, Paul Roberts, on Instagram.

    The candid monochrome picture saw her laughing as her husband gave her a sweet look. "Here is to love... here is to life... here is to you," Davis captioned the photo.

    Days after that, Davis posted another picture of her. Then, the World News Tonight host announced through the caption that she was working from home following COVID-19 exposure, and her husband was helping her set the home studio.

    Linsey and Paul Are Blessed with a Son

    The New York University graduate is blessed with a son named Ayden from her marriage with Roberts. Their son, who was born on March 28, 2014, celebrated his sixth birthday in 2020.

    Ayden is the apple of his parents' eyes. Davis often shares pictures of him on Instagram. She and Roberts make an effort to spend time with him, often engaging in fun activities like playing chess or boxing.

    Ayden is an inspiration to his mother. During a conversation with People on February 7, 2018, Davis shared how her son inspired her to write children’s books. 

    The University of Virginia alum has authored three children's books to date — 'The World Is Awake: A Celebration of Everyday Blessings' in 2018, 'One Big Heart' in 2019, and 'Stay This Way Forever' in 2021.

    Her first book, written to explain God to her son and toddlers, quickly became best-sellers.
