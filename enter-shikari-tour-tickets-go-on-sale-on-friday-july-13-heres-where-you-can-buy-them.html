    TICKETS for Enter Shikari’s winter tour go on sale on Friday July 13.

    The tour, which will support their latest album, The Spark, is the biggest the St-Albans four-piece have ever planned.

    At 9:00am the tickets will go on sale on ticketing platforms including AXS and Ticketmaster.

    25 dates are available across the UK, from Cardiff to Cambridge.

    • Find Enter Shikari's tour tickets at AXS here
