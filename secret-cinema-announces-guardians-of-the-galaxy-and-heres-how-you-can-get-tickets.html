    Secret Cinema announces Guardians of the Galaxy and here's how you can get tickets

    IF you haven't been to Secret Cinema yet, where have you been?

    And their latest collaboration, is a fully immersive show built on Marvel Studios’ Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.2.

    If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

    The first partnership between Secret Cinema and Disney, it's a must see for Marvel fans.

    Secret Cinema will take revellers on a cosmic adventure with a new experience built on the hit film.

    And just head of the release of Guardians of the Galaxy Volume 3 next year, there's never been a better time to see it.

    Tickets will be available from secretcinema.org here and will go on sale March 14 at 10am, and presale tickets through O2 Priority customers will go sale March 9 at 10am.

    For those eager to join the Ravagers, you can also get early access by signing up to the waitlist here.

    For the first time, Secret Cinema will also offer two types of tickets, one for a participatory experience with a screening of the film and another for a ticket focusing solely on the immersive experience.

    Tickets are priced from £47 plus booking fees for the immersive ticket option, and the full experience is priced from £59 plus booking fees; with premium tickets also priced from £98.

    In a secret location in London this August, Secret Cinema fans will be able to join the infamous Ravagers.

    Expect a mixtape of funk, spectacle and mischief as you travel across the universe.

    Max Alexander, CEO, Secret Cinema said: “Guardians of the Galaxy is an iconic franchise, and it’s been an absolute pleasure to work alongside the creative teams to bring the spectacular world to life in a new way.

    "Guests will be transported to the far reaches of the galaxy and experience an evening like no other as they interact with characters in a storyline unique to them against a backdrop of sensational special effects.

    "Whether they want to lead their Clan to resounding victory over their scavenger foes, or just to revel in the pleasures to be found on Contraxia, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.”

    Sarah Beers, VP, Franchise Creative & Marketing, Marvel Studios: “Marvel Studios strives to build intricate and fascinating worlds that fans can fully engage in, so we are beyond thrilled to be able to work with Secret Cinema on this project to bring the sights and sounds of a cosmic adventure to life in an immersive cinematic experience like no other.”
