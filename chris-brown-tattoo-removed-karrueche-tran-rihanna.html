    Rihanna: Remove The Tattoo… Or Else

    The painful laser surgeries that Chris is subjecting himself to are all in the name of love: “Rihanna was like, ‘You can’t have [Karrueche’s] face on your arm if you’re with me,” an insider told Media Takeout. If Chris refused to get it removed, Rihanna reportedly would’ve dumped him!

    The demand is pretty understandable — no girl wants to be held by arms filled with ex-girlfriends’ faces. But we hope that Rihanna doesn’t demand that Chris fill the void with a tattoo dedicated to her.

    Look Chris, it’s a bad idea! Tattoos are permanent — relationships aren’t! Especially when it comes to you. Our advice is to keep your girlfriends off your body in the future.

    Chris Brown & Rihanna — Back Together For Good?

    Chris’ tattoo removal seems to be the final nail in making his reconciliation with Rihanna official.

    On Feb. 24, the “Don’t Wake Me Up” singer told The Mirror that he and Rihanna had completely made up: “She loves me, what can I say?” In the same interview Chris said, “I’m forgiven… but yes, I worked hard for it.”

    Continuing to gloat, Chris spoke with New York Post’s Page Six and told them, “We are back together. She is the most beautiful girl in the world.”

    Take notes, all you guys trying to get your girlfriends back — throw her a lavish birthday party, write her a sweet love letter, and get that tattoo of your ex-girlfriend removed.
