    NORWICH Airport has been closed due to "unsafe levels" of snow.

    Staff closed the airport at 10.45am and will reassess the situation in the next couple of hours.

    It is not known when the transport hub is expected to reopen.

    Passengers are being urged to check the latest information before heading there.

    Pilot Lee Sutton shared a photo of the snow-covered runway on Twitter this morning.
