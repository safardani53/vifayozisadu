    Rebel Wilson just announced her engagement to Ramona Agruma, her partner. The former shared two Instagram photos of Agruma showing off her engagement ring as she and Wilson kissed in the background. This was followed by another photo of the couple standing for the camera with Sleeping Beauty’s castle at Disneyland in the backdrop and smiling. The caption read:

    “Yeah, we said! Thank you for the beautiful ring, @tiffanyandco, and to Bob Iger and the great crew at Disneyland @disneyweddings for pulling off this spectacular surprise!”

    Ramona Agruma is a well-known fashion designer.

    Ramona Agruma, 37, was born on January 29, 1986. The fashion designer is best known as the creator of the clothing line Lemon Ve Limon, a sustainable clothing line situated in Los Angeles that aspires to provide its clients with comfort and style. Agruma is presently a brand ambassador for the watch and jewelry company Bee Goddess. Bee Goddess’ products have also been utilized by celebrities such as Kate Winslet. Ramona also founded and is the creative director of the jewelry firm DeLys. The brand has a selection of large diamonds in various forms that have been worn by well-known faces in the entertainment sector.

    Agruma is active on Instagram, where she has approximately 80k followers and has posted 785 times. Her photos typically show her in natural settings, and she has been spotted posing alongside Rebel Wilson on a few occasions. Thus yet, no detailed information regarding her upbringing or educational history has been released.

    Rebel Wilson and Ramona Agruma’s dating history

    Although it is unknown how Rebel Wilson and Ramona Agruma originally met, Rebel confirmed to a news source last year that she has a special someone in her life. Rebel said in her interview that she and Ramona chatted over the phone for some time, which she described as romantic and also a method for them to get to know one another. Wilson said at the time that it was fantastic to have someone who treats everyone as an equal partner.

    Rebel went on to say that while she was with her ex-boyfriends, she was putting up walls that she shouldn’t have, and that it felt great to be in a good relationship. Last year, Rebel and Ramona were seen together at the Vanity Fair Oscars party as well as at Operation Smile’s annual Park City Ski Challenge charity event in Utah. Wilson confirmed her connection with Agruma and came out at the same time in June 2022.
