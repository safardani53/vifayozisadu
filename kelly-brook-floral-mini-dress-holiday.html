    KELLY Brook looked stunned in a pink floral mini-dress as she returned to work after her holiday to Italy.

    The 43-year-old radio host beamed as she was snapped in London wearing the eye-catching garment.

    Former model Kelly didn't let the bad weather dampen her spirits while showing off her curves in the clingy material.

    Kelly, who hosts Heart Drive with Jason King aka JK, recently jetted to Sardinia to celebrate her first wedding anniversary with husband Jeremy Parisi.

    In snaps shared on Instagram, the couple looked happier than ever while soaking up the sun during a yacht trip.

    Kelly recently opened up about her marriage during an appearance on Loose Women.

    Asked if getting married had changed their 10 year relationship, Kelly replied: "I definitely think that we're looking forward now. And what that looks like for us.

    "Because he is Italian and he's got family abroad. And you know, everything was kind of my way for a long time. And it's now about you know, compromising.

    "It's like not always spending summers with my family in Kent it's like spending more summers abroad and being with him and his family.

    She added: "He's got a place in Italy. And we got married there which was was really beautiful. Yeah, really nice. He's a lovely guy. And he's, you know, he's comes from like a farming background. So he's very normal."
